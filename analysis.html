SWOT Analysis

Internal Strengths Weaknesses
  • Website's Current Design
    • We should keep the color format of blue and yellow since these are the primary colors for the Blockbuster logo
    • The simplicity of this website is very user-friendly which makes accecibility stress free.
    • The responsivness, making the webiste easily visible on all device our customers own today.
  • Positive Organization Traits
    • We can highlight the products we are selling by displaying some images of our present-day customers wearing our merchandise.
    • We want our future customers to remember why they are nostalgic about our blockbuster merchandise when they see it on our website.
    • We want to display the famly friendly culture that the company presents to it's customers and employees.
  • Positive Reputation
    • We are known for being the last blockbuster.
    • What make us different from our competitors is that any local customer can pay to rent a physical copy of a Blu-ray or DVD at our in-store location.
    • We also provide custom made blockbuster merchandise to support our in-store location financially which anyone can buy outside of our location through our website.
  • Accecibility
    • Since we are only a one location store it will be hard to promote and access our company outside of Oregon.
    • We do not provide a streaming service for our customers like our competitors.
    • We only provide physical copies which can be hard to promote during COVID-19.
  • Complaints
    • The outdated design of the current website.
    • Showing the accredited critics on the homepage but not including posite quotes from the source.
    • Not having a more interactive site that encourages the user to visit in the future.
  • Non-existent Feature
    • User-generated content on the website that showcases our customers with our products on their social media sites.
    • We want to display the positive quotes we have received from highly regarded sources on our website.
    • We want to add movie trailers to the invidual movie pages near the movie information.
External Opportunities Threats
  • Last Location
    • The markets and economy forced almost all of the Blockbuster locations to close except for one, the location in Bend, Oregon.
    • Although a huge loss, this makes the company even more special.
    • Considering it’s the last remaining store, it is important that it is emphasized in the design of the site to encourage visiting and purchasing of merchandise.
  • Poplutation Changes
    • The population in Bend, Oregon is fairly small compared to most cities but offers unique nature resorts and trails that can attract more visitors.
    • The age of the nationwide population that grew up with Blockbuster in its prime are at the age in which traveling and visiting such sites is a “thing.”
    • The amount of people that grew up in the age of Blockbust now have extended families to share this experience with.
  • Technology Changes
    • The continuous development of the smartphone and their applications will propel the goals of the company.
    • The conveniency of being able to access the Blockbuster website for the latest news and merchandise.
    • The use of social platforms can help propel the company by reaching out to audiences across the nation and we will provide a website that will prompt users to partake in what we have to offer.
  • Travel
    • The effects of COVID-19 to reduced traveling and loss of jobs.
    • People not having easy access to the only location.
    • Unable to bring the family along for the trip due to COVID-19 occupancy limitations.
  • Other Options
    • Popular outside vendors selling Blockbuster merchandise and replication of the “Blockbuster” experience.
    • People can still rent movies from other streaming sources or purchase their own copies from thier local Walmart or Target.
    • Since the advancements in technology are simplifying life people are less interested in leaving the house.
  • Limitations
    • Renting out the store for Airbnb specials can increase the chances of COVID-19.
    • There can be liability issues from renting out the only location.
    • The current Airbnb special is only for a limited time during the dates Sept 18-20.
